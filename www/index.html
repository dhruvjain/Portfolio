Few Projects and Research Work in Showcase

Personality Analysis Based On Community Q&A sites

Project link

Personality Analysis Based On Community Q&A sites
Guide: Prof. Pawan Goyal

It is a part of Natural Language and Processing Project. This project aims at identifying the personality of Quora users with respect to the big five personality traits (Extroversion, Openness, Conscientiousness, Agreeableness, Neuroticism) using linguistic features based analysis of their answers.

We worked on identifying linguistic as well as Psychological Features to characterize Personality and using Classification techniques like SMO, Logistic Regression and many more to build the classifier. Expanded LIWC adding Quora specific features

The project got among top 4 teams in competition organized by Flipkart

QoS/QoE provisioning for Smartphone OTT video Applications
Video QoE
Guide: Prof. Sandip Chakraborty

In this Project we explore the traffic characteristics of mobile streaming video apps, by considering YouTube Android app as a use case. We show that by observing the traffic pattern, we can predict possible video quality degradation and video re-buffering events. We develop a methodology for early prediction of possible re-buffering

Our work got accepted as Poster Paper Publication in COMSNETS 2016 and we won BEST POSTER PAPER AWARD
